# 60-Second Friend Demo Script

## 0-10 seconds: Open the map (already nearby)
What they see:
- The map opens and centers on a nearby rare player ping close to your current location.
- The app quickly requests GPS access.
  - if yes, we continue in tracking mode with a realistic nearby starting distance.
  - if no, we show a static map and continue the story from there.

What you say:
"I’m starting near this rare player, so I only need to close the last bit of distance."

## 10-25 seconds: Explain proximity and constraints
What they see:
- The card shows rarity, distance, location, and success chance.
- The recruit button stays disabled until both range and GPS quality are good.
- Distance may start around 30–60m to match real-world pace.

What you say:
"To recruit, I need enough proximity and stable GPS. I’m making small position adjustments, not running hard."

## 25-45 seconds: Move toward the target
What they see:
- Distance/range state improves.
- The recruit button becomes ready.

What you say:
"I’m close enough now and the signal is good. This is the recruit window."

## 45-60 seconds: Attempt the recruit
What they see:
- Tap Recruit.
- Show success or failure.
- Toast/message appears.

What you say if success:
"Got him. He’s now in my roster."

What you say if failure:
"Missed him, but the sighting is still active, so I can try again before he disappears."
